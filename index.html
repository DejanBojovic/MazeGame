Info

Welcome to this humble maze game. Its mechanism relies on drag and drop functionality. There is one player and one target. But pay attention! You can hit walls and lose.

Useful tip: When dragging the pin you're playing with(top right corner) press and hold the middle of the white circle.

Easy mode: you have as much time as you need!
Medium mode: you have ten seconds to finish!
Hard mode: you have five seconds to finish and the walls are moving!

Good luck stranger!
